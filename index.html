Install
Copy & Paste the stylesheet <link> into your <head> before all other stylesheets. 
            <!-- Lune CSS -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/uroki/lune@1.0.0/dist/lune.min.css" />
Place the following <script> near the end of your pages, right before the closing </body> tag. 
            <!-- Lune JS -->
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/uroki/lune@1.0.0/dist/lune.min.js"></script>
